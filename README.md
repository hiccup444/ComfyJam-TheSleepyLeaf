
# The Sleepy Leaf

  [Itch.io](https://jameskjams2learnhelp.itch.io/the-sleepy-leaf)

**Welcome to The Sleepy Leaf**  – a cozy tea shop tucked inside a hollow autumn tree, where woodland creatures line up for warmth before hibernation.

**ALSO WORKS ON TABLETS!**

As the cold sets in, anxious squirrels, polite badgers, and sleepy bears stop by for the perfect cup of tea. Hand-craft custom brews, manage your tiny shop, and help the forest unwind before winter.

  # Features

-   **Physics-based tea making**  
    Tear packets, dip and swirl teabags with string physics, pour milk with a gentle tilt, and top drinks right on the counter.
    
-   **Woodland regulars**  
    Serve forest animals with their own personalities, patience levels, and favorite drinks.
    
-   **148+ recipe combinations**  
    Mix hot teas, iced drinks, milk varieties, and seasonal toppings to match each order exactly.
    
-   **Day-by-day shop life**  
    Earn money, watch the clock from golden morning to starlit night, and keep up as expectations grow.
    
-   **Cozy autumn atmosphere**  
    Hand-drawn art, gentle wind, a crackling fireplace, and a soft, sleepy fall vibe.
    
-   **Short, replayable days**  
    Perfect for a relaxing session with comfy tea-making and light management.

# Controls

-   **1 / 2 / 3**  – Switch between shop stations
    
-   **Right Mouse Button**  – Interact (pick up, pour, use objects)
    
-   **Move mouse to screen edge**  – Pan between stations
    
Pour a cup, take a breath, and help the forest prepare for winter—one perfect brew at a time.
